TRAINING PLAN FOR:

KEEP TO A GOOD STANDARD:

TRAINING: Overall Winrate

Tips:

  • To win more games, figure out your strengths and weaknesses and use it to your advantage.
  • Practice several different roles and champions to play when you can't play your main.

Video Guides:

TRAINING: Damage Dealing

Tips:

  • Learn how to harass/trade effectively, doing so can snowball you to victory.
  • Every champion in LoL has potential for high damage when played right, learn how your main can deal damage.

Video Guides:

TRAINING: Damage Taking

Tips:

  • Every skillshot in LoL can be dodged, always bare in mind what abilities your enemy has and try to predict their next move.
  • Learn when to play safe, often staying out of the enemies’ auto-attack range is enough to avoid harass and taking damage.

Video Guides:

TRAINING: Creep Score

Tips:

  • Being able to farm effectively is the biggest difference between high and low level players.
  • There is an opportunity for mastery within farming, practice makes perfect, try a custom game with no distractions.

Video Guides:

TRAINING: Gold per Minute

Tips:

  • The faster gold is earned directly improves your stats and abilities, learning how to efficiently earn gold is key.
  • Learn the best way to earn gold for your role and specialise in the mechanics of it.

Video Guides:

TRAINING: Kills per Game

Tips:

  • Learn the damage your auto-attack does at every level and know when you can kill secure without any danger.
  • Using a summoner spell is often the best way to get more kills, using Flash tactically or Ignite for pure damage to kill the enemy.

Video Guides:

TRAINING: Assists per Game

Tips:

  • Getting the whole team involved in a team fight means there is no chance the enemy can out-number you.
  • f each member of your team does one auto attack to an enemy, it has the potential to do half the health of tanks or almost kill squishies.

Video Guides:

TRAINING: Deaths per Game

Tips:

  • Death timers are the biggest hinderance to winning games, the later you die in the game the longer the death timer.
  • Remembering when your enemies’ summoner spells are up can help you avoid an unexpected death in lane.

Video Guides:

TRAINING: Turrets per Game

Tips:

  • Sometimes dying for a tower is worth it, the pressure you can apply with a destroyed tower can win a lane.
  • Some champions such as Ziggs deal bonus damage to towers, always look out for them on the enemy team and play accordingly.
